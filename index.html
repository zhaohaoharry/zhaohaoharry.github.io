Environmental and Experimental Economics

Hao Zhao (赵浩)

Associate Professor, School of Ecology and Environment, Renmin University of China

I work on environmental and resource economics, with a parallel research program in laboratory market design. My current projects study emissions trading, green innovation, water markets, and the design of trading institutions under information and stock externalities.

Current experiment

DCA Experiments

The current experiment program studies token reallocation under three market institutions: a uniform-price consignment auction, a continuous double auction, and a two-sided consignment auction with a clinching mechanism.

Students log in with a subject ID and passcode, read the experiment introduction, complete the quiz, and then enter repeated trading rounds. The experiment page provides the current access instructions and student login entry point.
